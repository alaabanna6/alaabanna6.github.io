About Me

Designing with purpose.

I'm a product designer who believes great design starts with deep empathy. My process combines UX research, information architecture, and high-fidelity prototyping, grounded in real user needs and driven by AI automation.

AI automation isn't a shortcut for me, it's how I work. I use it to accelerate ideation, build smarter workflows, and push the boundaries of what a designer can ship alone. I stay obsessively up to date with the latest in AI because in this field, what's possible changes every week.

Currently completing a Master of Digital Media at Toronto Metropolitan University, deepening my research at the intersection of product design, AI, and human experience.
