Overview

Project Overview

Monstrum is an AI-powered content growth engine that automates content creation, optimization, and publishing across SEO, AEO, and GEO for businesses and agencies. As Lead Product Designer, I am responsible for the full product experience — both the end-client facing flows and the internal engine dashboard that exposes the 12-agent pipeline. This project uses agentic AI workflows including Claude Code and Figma MCP to accelerate research, iteration, and design decisions.

Focus Areas

What I'm Designing

End-Client Product Experience

Designing the full client-facing flow — from the main dashboard and AI Citation Score overview to content review and approval, content calendar, and performance drill-down.

Internal Engine Dashboard

Designing the internal interface that exposes the 12-agent pipeline — giving the team visibility into content research, blueprint generation, writing, optimization, publishing, and monitoring.

AI-Powered Design Process

Using Claude Code and Figma MCP to automate research synthesis, generate design briefs, and accelerate every stage of the design process from insight to prototype.
